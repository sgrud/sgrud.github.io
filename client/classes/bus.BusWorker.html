Class BusWorker

The BusWorker is a background Thread which is Spawned by the BusHandler to handle all published and observed streams, uplinks and their aggregation depending on their hierarchy.

uplink

  • uplink(handle, url): Promise<Subscription>

  • Invoking this method uplinks the supplied handle to the supplied url by establishing a WebSocket connection between the endpoint behind the supplied url and this BusWorker. It is assumed, that all messages emanating from the WebSocket endpoint conform to the Bus.Value type and are therefore treated as such. This treatment includes the filtering of all received and submitted messages by comparing their corresponding Bus.Handle and the supplied handle.

    Parameters

    • handle: Handle

      The Bus.Handle to uplink.

    • url: string
